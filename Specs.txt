Flight zmanim calculator

Who uses it?
People flying who are orthodox jews and need to know when to pray

Why does he use it? What is he trying to accomplish when he uses it?
To know when certain halachic times are, and we will include more zmanim than given by traditional zmanim calculators

What functions does your software provide to the user that help him achieve his goal(s)?
It will tell you what time to pray, as above.

How does he use it? What steps does he go through in order to achieve his goal(s)? What are the workflows he progresses through when using it?
He must enter the flight information, where he left, date and time. Or, he can insert a flightpath from flightaware.com.
Then press calculate, and the software will tell you how much time into the flight any relevant zman is at.
